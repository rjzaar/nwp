maintainable system in just 3 weeks. Key turning points were:

1. Jan 3:  Security crisis → Two-tier secrets architecture

4. Jan 12: Major restructure → Professional 8-folder system

This timeline demonstrates:
- Rapid response to security concerns
- Documentation-driven development
- Regular audit and consolidation cycles
- Meta-documentation as a valuable practice
- Evolution from ad-hoc to professional in 3 weeks
